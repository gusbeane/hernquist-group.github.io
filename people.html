People

There are many brilliant postdocs and students in our group. Lars is a primary advisor for N Graduate students and M Postdoctoral Fellows.

Ph.D. Students

Magdalena Siwek

Magdalena (G-6) develops numerical simulations of circumbinary disks (CBDs), and study the effect of CBD driven binary evolution on EM transients and GW source populations.

Angus "Gus" Beane

Gus (G-5) works on controlled simulations of galaxies, with a particular focus on understanding the formation and evolution of the Milky Way.

Ana Maria Delgado

Ana Maria's (G-4) interests lie in cosmological simulations and Machine Learning.

Mouza Almualla

Mouza (G-2) is studying Line intensity mapping and Lyman alpha forst using THESAN simulations.

Olga Borodina

Olga (G-2) runs hydrodynamical simulations of jets in the galactic center that propogate through interstellar medium and cause galaxy quenching.

PostDocs
